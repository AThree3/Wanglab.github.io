Introduction

Welcome to WANGLab, in the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at Temple University. We foster an open, motivated, creative, and team-oriented culture in our group. Welcome all scholars and students to join our lab.

Research

Our research focuses on Mobile Sensing, Internet of Things, Cyber Security and Privacy, Artificial Intelligence and Sotfware & Hareware Co-Design. Our work is motivated by a wide variety of engineering and applications.
